About Us

Welcome to Tazza, your one-stop destination for quality groceries delivered right to your doorstep. At Tazza, we are committed to providing you with the freshest and finest selection of groceries, along with exceptional customer service.

Our Story

Tazza was founded with a simple yet ambitious goal - to make grocery shopping convenient, affordable, and enjoyable for our valued customers. Our journey began in 1994 when Maria - founder - saw a need for a more efficient and customer-centric grocery shopping experience. Armed with a passion for quality products and a dedication to service excellence, Maria launched Tazza to bring the best of the grocery store directly to your home.
